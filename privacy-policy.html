Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pin High (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use our website and related services.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve Pin High.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate the service, and when required by law.

4. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of certain personal information, subject to legal limits.
  • You may opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

7. Children’s Privacy

Pin High is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date when changes are made.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@pinhigh.app.
